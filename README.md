# 🌿 Shivmay Bharat Mission

## Our Mission

**Shivmay Bharat Mission** is a nationwide environmental initiative launched by *Prakriti Foundation* to promote tree plantation across India, with a special focus on planting **Belpatra trees** in front of homes.

This mission blends **environmental conservation** with **spiritual awareness**, encouraging citizens to reconnect with nature while contributing to a healthier and greener planet.

---

## 🌱 Vision

To create an India that is:
- 🌳 **Green** through widespread tree plantation  
- 🧹 **Clean** through environmental responsibility  
- 🕉️ **Spiritually vibrant** through cultural and natural harmony  

---

## 🌍 What Makes This Mission Unique?

- Combines **ecological sustainability** with **spiritual values**
- Encourages planting of **Belpatra trees**, which hold cultural and environmental significance
- Promotes **community participation** across all sections of society
